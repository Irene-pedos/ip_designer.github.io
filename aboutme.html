Frequently Asked Questions

What web development services do you offer?
We offer full-stack web development including front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (Node.js, PHP) development, as well as CMS solutions like WordPress.
What's included in your logo design service?
Our logo design package includes initial concepts, revisions, and final delivery in multiple formats (PNG, JPG, SVG, AI). We provide 3 initial concepts and 2 rounds of revisions.
What types of video editing do you specialize in?
We specialize in promotional videos, social media content, YouTube videos, and corporate presentations. We can handle color correction, audio enhancement, and special effects.
What file formats will I receive for my flyer/poster?
We deliver print-ready PDFs (CMYK) for professional printing, as well as web-optimized JPG/PNG versions for digital use. Source files (AI/PSD) are available upon request.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by project: logos take 3-7 days, websites 2-6 weeks, video editing 1-2 weeks, and flyers/posters 3-5 days. Rush services are available for an additional fee.
What are your payment terms?
We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone payments.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes! We include 2 rounds of revisions in all our packages. Additional revisions can be arranged for a small fee.
How do I get started?
Simply contact us via email or phone to discuss your project. We'll provide a free consultation and quote with no obligation.
